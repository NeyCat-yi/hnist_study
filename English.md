## unit 1

*The use of **biodegradable** plastics that can break down naturally can reduce the accumulation of environmentally persistent plastic wastes.*

使用可自然分解的**可生物降解**塑料可以减少环境中持久性塑料废物的积累。

------

*Smaller, lighter, and nimbler (更灵活的) than its **predecessors**, the newly designed robotic arm can be programmed to perform a wide variety of tasks with great accuracy.*

与之前的机器人相比，新设计的机械臂更小、更轻、更灵活，可以编程执行各种各样的任务，而且精度很高。

------

*When new technologies reach a mature stage at which they can be used for business, they have the potential to rapidly change the economic landscape far beyond what could have been expected at the **infancy** stage of innovation.*

当新技术发展到可以应用于商业的成熟阶段时，它们有可能迅速改变经济格局，其影响远远超出创新初期阶段的预期。

------

*The flying car concept, which existed only in the realms of science fiction, is to become a reality in the not-too-distant future and **revolutionize** urban transportation and parcel delivery services.*

曾经只存在于科幻小说中的飞行汽车概念，将在不久的将来成为现实，并彻底改变城市交通和包裹递送服务。

------

*The **scope** of the study was broad: it took into account such variables as age, gender, hours per week spent online, and years of online use.*

该研究的**范围**很广：它考虑了年龄、性别、每周上网时间以及上网年限等变量。

------

我对**研究结果**感到惊讶，研究表明，大多数人夸大了自己的信息，隐瞒了信息，在某些情况下，甚至发布了不真实的信息。

------

*People may **procrastinate** when they are confused by the complexity of a task or when they're overly distracted or fatigued.*

当人们被任务的复杂性弄得不知所措，或者过度分心或疲惫时，他们可能会**拖延**。

------

*Each person's experience is different, but over the course of the lifespan, **self-esteem** seems to rise and fall in predictable, systematic ways.*

每个人的经历都不同，但从长远来看，**自尊**似乎会以可预测的、系统的方式起伏。

## unit 2

*Wabi-sabi is an Eastern philosophy that **refers to** the ability to see the beauty in imperfections, which manifests (显现，显露) itself both in daily life activity as making home pottery, and in spiritual experience of opening up inner visions that can change a life.*

*侘寂*是一种东方哲学，**指的是**在不完美中发现美的能力，这种能力既体现在日常生活中，例如制作家用陶器，也体现在精神体验中，例如开启内在的视野，从而改变人生。

*Xieyi (写意) is a looser style of Chinese painting, usually used in landscapes, **featuring** exaggerated forms and expressing the artist's personality and character.*

*写意*是中国绘画中一种较为自由的风格，通常用于山水画，其特点是**夸张的**造型，并表达艺术家的个性和性格。

*Originating in China, the earliest porcelains (瓷器) used a special type of clay and a type of granite (花岗岩) while early European **versions** used clay and glass.*

瓷器起源于中国，最早的瓷器使用一种特殊的粘土和一种花岗岩，而早期的欧洲瓷器则使用粘土和玻璃。

*The Japanese art of kintsugi teaches that broken objects are not something to hide but to display with pride because the value of pottery is actually **enhanced** thanks to its "scars."*

日本的金缮艺术告诉我们，破损的物品不应该被隐藏，而应该自豪地展示出来，因为陶器的价值实际上会因其“伤痕”而**增强**。

*The mechanical advantage of a machine is the ability to work efficiently and effectively, especially to do tasks that are difficult for humans such as lifting **thick planks** or pushing heavy things.*

机器的机械优势是指机器能够高效、有效地工作，尤其是在完成人类难以完成的任务时，例如举起**厚木板**或推动重物。

*Although they did not intend to **harm** the local traditions, what happened in effect was the disappearance of traditional **craftsmanship**.*

虽然他们并非有意**伤害**当地传统，但实际上却导致了传统**工艺**的消失。

***Keeping on good terms with** local people will help the company to survive overseas.*

与当地人保持良好关系有助于公司在海外生存。

*Whether acquired by trade or by gift, these **handcrafts** were treasured throughout the Middle Ages (中世纪) as marvels of imaginative craftsmanship and were often imitated.*

无论是通过交易还是礼物获得的这些**手工艺品**，在中世纪都被视为富有想象力的工艺奇迹而备受珍视，并经常被模仿。

## unit 3

*Most people with healthy immunity (免疫力) are not easily **affected** by bacteria on the skin, but people with a weakened immune system are more at risk.*

大多数免疫力健康的人不容易受到皮肤上细菌的**影响**，但免疫系统较弱的人更容易受到感染。

*Globally healthcare professionals are **convinced** that technologies like personal health monitoring devices will play a vital role in creating a positive healthcare future.*

全球医疗保健专业人士普遍认为，个人健康监测设备等技术将在创造积极的医疗保健未来中发挥至关重要的作用。

*Notably, early risk detection on the **outbreak** of the infectious disease will help to impose timely and effective public health measures to prevent its spread.*

值得注意的是，及早发现传染病爆发的风险将有助于及时有效地采取公共卫生措施，防止其传播。

*Medical researchers **proposed** a new disease category of skin disorders.*

医学研究人员提出了一种新的皮肤疾病类别。

*Further analysis revealed **significant** differences between the control group and the treatment group.*

进一步分析显示，对照组和治疗组之间存在**显著**差异。

*Carrying excess weight has an adverse **consequence** for one's health and increases the risk of health problems.*

体重过重会对人的健康产生不利影响，并增加患疾病的风险。

*The main objectives of this reform were to reduce health **expenditure** for patients, and improve the quality of hospital services.*

这项改革的主要目标是降低患者的医疗**支出**，并提高医院服务质量。

*It is **estimated** that by 2050, there will be nearly 13.8 million Americans age 65 and older that will suffer from Alzheimer's (阿尔茨海默病).*

据**估计**，到 2050 年，将有近 1380 万 65 岁及以上的美国人患有阿尔茨海默病。

*The government funded a new media campaign to raise people's awareness and concern about autism (自闭症).*

政府资助了一项新的媒体宣传活动，以提高人们对自闭症的认识和关注。

## unit 4

*Early childhood educators face huge challenges when they work with children and families from culturally and **linguistically** diverse backgrounds.*

幼儿教育工作者在与来自文化和语言背景各异的儿童和家庭一起工作时，面临着巨大的挑战。

*Strong government commitment and leadership are needed to **enforce** effective measures to protect cultural heritage from multiple threats.*

需要政府强有力的承诺和领导，才能**实施**有效措施，保护文化遗产免受多种威胁。

*Linguists studied **approximately** 37,000 words across five different languages (English, Spanish, Dutch, German, and Polish) to figure out the connection between the sounds that were pronounced and the emotions that were expressed in all five languages.*

语言学家研究了五种不同语言（英语、西班牙语、荷兰语、德语和波兰语）中约 37,000 个单词，以找出这五种语言中发音与表达的情感之间的联系。

*In a flipped (翻转的) class, the traditional teaching process is **reversed** with class time dedicated to discussion-based activities, whereas learners construct their own knowledge outside of the classroom.*

在翻转课堂中，传统的教学过程被**颠倒**，课堂时间用于讨论式活动，而学习者则在课堂外构建自己的知识。

*Experiences of **discrimination** can trigger negative emotions that can lead to altered physiological reactions, which can increase the risk of poor health.*

遭受歧视的经历会引发负面情绪，进而导致生理反应改变，从而增加健康状况不佳的风险。

*Ongoing urbanization has the potential to **exacerbate** bee declines, unless measures are taken to make sure appropriate floral (花卉的) resources are available.*

持续的城市化有可能加剧蜜蜂数量的下降，除非采取措施确保有足够的鲜花资源。

*A research indicates about 100 to 2,500 bird species may go **extinct** due to climate change, depending on the severity of global warming and habitat loss, and on the ability of birds to find new homes as rising temperatures force them to higher elevations (海拔) where there is less or no habitat.*

一项研究表明，由于气候变化，大约有 100 到 2500 种鸟类可能会灭绝，这取决于全球变暖和栖息地丧失的严重程度，以及鸟类在气温上升迫使它们迁徙到更高海拔地区（那里栖息地较少或没有）寻找新家园的能力。

*Some species lack the traits needed to achieve dominance across a range of habitats at all which would make them **intrinsically** subordinate (从属的).*

有些物种缺乏在各种栖息地中取得优势所需的特征，这使得它们本质上是从属的。

*Thanks to the Chinese government's national **conservation** program for the giant panda and its habitat, the number of panda reserves has already drastically increased.*

得益于中国政府针对大熊猫及其栖息地的国家级**保护**计划，大熊猫保护区的数量已经大幅增加。

管理层定义了组织文化如何影响员工、客户以及组织如何处理**道德**问题的框架。

如今，多层面维和行动被用来维护国际和平与安全。

当我们漫不经心地在网上发布自己的生活时，我们的数据被科技公司共享、交易和使用，这引发了关于数字数据收集伦理的日益激烈的**争议性**问题。

阻碍公司发展的未必是公司员工的违法行为，而是有时发生的、未被察觉的**轻微违法行为**。

被判犯有逃税罪的个人或实体可能会受到严厉的处罚，包括罚款和数月甚至数年的监禁。

*Scholars have defined a corporate moral **code** as a set of standards used to guide the behavior of both employees and the company.*

学者们将企业道德准则定义为一套用于指导员工和公司行为的标准。

*Some people treat rewards as the most powerful **motivators** that help in **regulating** one's behavior and emotions in the workplace.*

有些人认为奖励是最强大的**激励因素**，有助于**调节**一个人在工作场所的行为和情绪。
